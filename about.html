Hi, I'm Karina Bittencourt

a passionate photographer on a visual journey to capture moments infused with emotion. I find inspiration in the beauty of everyday life and the extraordinary nuances that often go unnoticed.

Continuing my photography journey since 2011 has been a winding road filled with both challenges and unyielding passion. As I embarked on my college studies in photography, the excitement of capturing moments through my lens became a central part of my identity. However, life took an unexpected turn, and I found myself needing to prioritize family responsibilities over completing my degree. Despite the detour, my love for photography never wavered. I persisted in refining my skills, finding comfort and joy in the act of storytelling through images.

Moreover, my love for travel and the great outdoors continues to influence my photography, infusing it with a sense of adventure and a deep appreciation for the beauty that surrounds us.

My life journey has led me to reside in various places. Each relocation, every interaction, and the shifting landscapes contribute to the unfolding narrative of my work. The prospect of meeting and collaborating with new clients fills me with excitement, infusing my portfolio with fresh perspectives and stories. Having recently established myself in the welcoming community of Colorado, I am enthusiastic about connecting with clients in the area.
